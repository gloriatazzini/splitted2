«Who the hell are they?» I ask to the rioter beside me, as we hide ourselves behind some chairs.

«I have no idea. But I heard someone talk about this in the market the other day. We should dig into it.»

These people scare me, and not because of their weapons. They are clearly Submitians, but they are not responding for themselves: some of them look at us with blank eyes, or with froth coming out of their mouth, shooting at us without seeing the fact that we are just like them.
Someone or something is clearly controlling their actions and behaviour.

«Do you think the priest has something to do with this?»

«He is certainly involved, I wouldn't be surprised if he put them there exactly to protect him. He probably knew we were coming. What a coward.»
