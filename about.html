SPLITTED IS AN INTERACTIVE NARRATIVE


It follows the story of three generations of people, struggling to live and survive in a suffocating city. As you go deeper in the narrative, you will get the chance to interact more and more with it.



INSPIRED BY SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT GOAL 11: “SUSTAINABLE CITIES AND COMMUNITIES”


This Goal is part of the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development and it's focused on “making cities and human settlements inclusive, safe, resilient and sustainable”. The story is set in Aether, a city that had to adapt to an unsustainable model: a vertical growth brought its areas to become more clustered based on the economical possibilities of its citizens. This division creates social and cultural discrepancies, creating frustration and troubles in its inhabitants.
