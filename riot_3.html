«Some people are implying that they shouldn't have been there. That if they hadn't been there, this tragedy would have been avoided. But let's not forget, beloved devotees, that everybody in this city has a role. And if you die fulfilling that role, you are doing the right thing.»

I turn to a guy beside me.
«Please tell me he is not talking about the miners.»

He looks at me, and in his eyes I see the same flames that burn in mine.
«Yes, he is. And he's gonna pay for this.»

People in the room nod in approval. Some of them even raise their hands in praise.

«Let's never forget, dear children of the compassionate Mother, that these roles bring order to the city. Now, I know you look around everyday, and order seems to be missing in Submitia. But it's with our hard work and dedication that we can keep this place from crumbling. WE are the foundations. WE are the load-bearing walls of our splendid cathedral of Aether. WE have the honour, and therefore the responsibility, to keep it from falling!»
