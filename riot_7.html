While we march towards the stage, I see different people standing up from the crowd and entering the room from the lateral doors: they start walking in our direction, and looking at them I notice their eyes are empty. They are all bald, men and women without distinction, and when I'm close enough I notice that on their scalp there's something tattooed.

I squint as I don't believe my eyes: the symbol they have tattooed on the head is the exact same I saw this afternoon on the back of the device I received. It's the exact same: the string, the halfmoon, the arrow.
They all walk towards us, keeping their hands on the belt where I notice a weapon hanging.

«Guys, they are armed!» I shout to the mates beside me. Some of them don't mind, and keep marching towards the stage. The armed men pull out the weapons and start shooting rubber bullets and tear gas at us. The chaos is triggered: submitians start panicking and running away from the room, passing beside us to reach the gates of the stairs.
