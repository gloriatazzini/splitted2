«May 14th, 4.34 pm - Mother Gloom’s Sacred Church»


When I arrive to the church, two ladies are speaking with Aibak. He thanks them profusely for their offers, with a paternalistic attitude I find revolting. He treats them all with a kindness that comes from an opportunistic and fake place, and manages to convince them all of his good heart and intentions.
When he sees me, the situation changes.

He dismisses them with a certain agitation, and when they are gone from the room he turns to me.

«Hello. What do you need?»
He seems prudent, almost afraid of me.

«I'm just here to give you some money» I leave the staters on the table, inside the bucket he has prepared for them. He seems reliefed by my answer, but I'm definetely not done with him. «And ask you, if you don't mind, what's about this symbol right here?»
