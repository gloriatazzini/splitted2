«Who's this girl Mayhar?»

«I tell you Mith, I have never seen her before.» he tells her, then he turns back at me «I'm sorry, were you at the riot yesterday? I really don't know to what information you are referring to.»

«Ehm, we talked during the riot, remember? You told me we would have to dig a little in this matter?»

He shakes his head in sincere apology. «I'm really sorry, what riot?»

The girl looks at him, with a new worried expression on his face. Now that I see them standing together, I notice an incredible resemblance between them: they must be brother and sister.

«What do you mean "what riot?" you dumbass, you left me here working a double shift because you had to go riot for my rights, for which I thank you very much, you were not helpful at all...»
