May 13th, 12.45 am - Public school of the 15th area


«Hey red!» I roll my eyes as Lisa Reyes stands in front of me in the hallway, trying to slow me down «we're organizing a protest at the weirdo's church tonight. You'll be there, right?»

Lisa, always up for trouble. I always try to avoid her, but I must come across as a revolutionary spirit, because she's always implicitly counting on me whenever matters like this pop up. She is the leader of a small group of guys in my class that everyone labeled as "punk", which is definetely fitting for them, but that's not the reason why I try to keep my distance: I do that with anybody, I just don't like being implicated in anyone's business. And she has a lot of business going on.
